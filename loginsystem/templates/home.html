Welcome to the Number Guessing Game

In the number guessing game, players attempt to guess a secret number chosen by the computer. The game has three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard. Each level features different ranges for the secret number and varying amounts of hints and attempts. The objective is to guess the number correctly within the allowed attempts while receiving feedback on whether guesses are too high or too low.

Easy

In the easy level of the number guessing game, players have 10 attempts to guess a secret number within a range of 1 to 100.

Medium

In the medium level of the number guessing game, players have 15 attempts to guess a secret number within a range of 1 to 150.

Hard

In the hard level of the number guessing game, players have 20 attempts to guess a secret number within a range of 1 to 200.
